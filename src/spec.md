# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a Smart Study Planner AI that helps students create personalized study schedules based on their subjects, exam dates, available study hours, and difficulty levels, with AI-powered schedule generation and progress tracking.

**Planned changes:**
- Create homepage with title, tagline, and navigation
- Build input form for subjects, exam dates, daily study hours, and difficulty levels
- Implement AI-based schedule generator that prioritizes subjects by exam date and difficulty, generates daily tasks, and suggests revision days
- Add progress tracker showing overall and per-subject completion percentages
- Include motivational quote section for student encouragement
- Design with soft colors, rounded corners, and calming aesthetic suitable for focused studying
- Ensure fully responsive layout for mobile, tablet, and desktop

**User-visible outcome:** Students can input their study requirements and receive an AI-generated personalized study schedule with daily tasks and revision days, track their progress across all subjects, and stay motivated with a beautiful, stress-free interface.
